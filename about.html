About us

About Matico

When I think of weaving, the image of my mother comes to mind, who, from the young age of 5, taught me the art of weaving.

Her name was Matilde, we affectionately called her Matico, I can remember the admiration I had for her artistic ability making the most beautiful garments. Stitch by stitch I learned from her the mastery of classic techniques such as crochet and two-needle knitting, later seeking to enrich my skills I learned the technique of frivolite among others.

Some time later I started as a hobby the elaboration of woven garments for adults and children which I made as a gift for friends and family, later before the good reception of my creations, I decided to start my own company, in honor of my mother, my example and my main mentor chose my brand name MATICO.

MATICO has among its catalog a large number of products for adults, children and babies, additionally it offers a wide range of home decoration and furniture, using only materials from the best suppliers such as lace, soft wool and threads combined with complex fabrics and patterns. In this way, MATICO products are unique, original, personalized and of the highest quality to always satisfy our customers.
